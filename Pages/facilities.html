Facilities

Dive into Fun with Our Free Swimming Pool

We offer a wonderful facility for those who love to swim. Our free swim facility is perfect for people of all ages who enjoy swimming as a form of exercise, relaxation, or just plain fun. We provide free swimming suits and have clear water, making your experience enjoyable. You can relax and have fun with your family and friends. Everyone is welcome to come and try our facility.

Free Breakfast Buffet

You can have your Free breakfast between 6Am to 9:30 Am. There are several things you can have as your breakfast or you can also have traditional foods.As we strive to provide the best possible experience for our guests, we prioritize your health and convenience, rendering cleanliness an implicit standard.

Enjoy Spa Facilities

Our spa facility offers a range of services to help you relax. We have a team of experienced and friendly helper who are providing you with a personalized experience. At our spa facility, we use only the highest quality products, so you can be sure that you are receiving the best possible care for your skin and body.
