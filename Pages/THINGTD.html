Inle lake

Inle lake is a peaceful lake with floating villages. And if you want to go somewhere you might need to go by boat. Some water sports are also held in the lake, occasionally.



Kalaw

Kalaw, which is located in Shan State surrounded by green forests, is known for its fresh air. The town is located at an altitude of around 1300 meters above sea level, making it a perfect retreat from the heat of lower Myanmar.

Yaypyar Lake

You don't have to go to the black sea to see the blue water. No need to travel to the Maldives beach. You don't have to play in the middle of Phuket beach. You can visit to the Village Blue Lake in Neltaung, Shan State, which is cheaper and more beautiful.

Mount Main Ma Ye

Main Ma Ye Thakinma Taung is nice pagoda on the mountain which can be seen from the side of the road. There are a number of pagoda on the top of the mountain. The view is very nice , amazing and beautiful more than what you expected.Good Hiking to the Mountain with couples.

Pin Ta Ya

Pindaya city is a picturesque town nestled amidst the rolling hills of Myanmar's Shan state.One of the main attractions in Pindaya is the Shwe U Min Cave, which is located at the base of the limestone ridge.
