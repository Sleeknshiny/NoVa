About Us

Welcome to our hotel! Our hotel is located in No.10, Pyi Htaung Su Road, AungBan, Kalw Township, Southern Shan State, Myanmar, with easy access to popular attractions and restaurants. We offer a range of comfortable and luxurious rooms, each designed with your comfort in mind. From check-in to check-out, we strive to make your stay with us as seamless and enjoyable as possible. We take pride in our commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility. We have implemented eco-friendly practices throughout our operations. We also offer a range of amenities and services to ensure that you have everything you need during your stay. We look forward to welcoming you and providing you with an unforgettable experience.
